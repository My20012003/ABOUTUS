about us

"What makes our clothes special?"

We strive to be a global leader in T-shirts and fashion outerwear by empowering innovation and design to provide total customer satisfaction. We strive to be a caring and well-managed organization for our business partners, customers and employees, and a responsible corporate citizen to our society.

To innovate, to lead, to enhance, to provide best-value products and services to global customers. To make a difference through our branding to stay ahead of fashion trends, market changes and the latest technology. To enhance the quality of life for our business partners,customers and employees.

"How does fashion represent our history?"

Founded in 2007 by José Neves for the love of fashion, and launched in 2008, we began as an e-commerce marketplace for luxury boutiques around the world. Today the Marketplace connects customers in over 190 countries and territories with items from more than 50 countries and over 1,400 of the world’s best brands, boutiques and department stores.
